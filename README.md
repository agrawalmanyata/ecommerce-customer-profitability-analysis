# E-Commerce Customer Profitability Analysis

## Business Problem

This project analyzes transactional, customer, and product data from an e-commerce business to evaluate operational efficiency, customer profitability, promotional effectiveness, and growth opportunities. The objective was to support business decision-making by identifying profitability drivers, assessing referral program performance, evaluating seasonal sales behavior, and recommending strategies for customer acquisition and operational optimization.

## Work Performed

The project involved large-scale data integration, data quality auditing, feature engineering, and business intelligence analysis using Python. Multiple datasets containing customer, product, and transaction information were cleaned, validated, and combined to create an integrated analytical dataset. Key analyses included hourly order volume trends, customer segment profitability, referral program ROI assessment, Black Friday sales impact evaluation, and geographic customer base analysis. The project also involved developing business recommendations, KPI frameworks, and performance monitoring strategies to support long-term growth.
